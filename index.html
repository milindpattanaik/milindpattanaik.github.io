About me

I am a 20-year-old Embedded & Systems Engineer with a strong passion for unmanned aerial systems (UAS) development. My expertise spans across UAV technologies, including ground control stations (GCS), flight controller firmware, and embedded systems, where I focus on building high-performance and reliable solutions for autonomous aerial platforms.

With deep expertise in embedded systems, I have extensive experience working with 8-bit AVR microcontrollers and 32-bit ARM Cortex-M processors. I specialize in low-level firmware development, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and embedded C/C++ programming, ensuring optimal efficiency and performance in mission-critical applications.

My interests extend into the defense and aerospace sectors, where I contribute to UAVs, guided missiles, rockets, and advanced autonomous systems. I am driven by innovation and dedicated to developing next-generation technologies that enhance security and operational capabilities.

Additionally, I am well-versed in PCB design and signal integrity analysis, focusing on high-speed digital and mixed-signal circuits. My experience includes designing custom flight controllers, sensor modules, and communication interfaces, ensuring robust and efficient hardware architectures.
